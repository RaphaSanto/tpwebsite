The Volkswagen Development Partner Forum, which took place in Wolfsburg on 17 and 18 April 2024, was an important source of inspiration for Teraport. This esteemed event served as a vital communication platform between Volkswagen and its development partners, offering a unique opportunity to share insights on new standards, trends, and strategies in the automotive industry.
The Partner Forum focused on enhancing understanding of product and system decisions and application methodologies through informative presentations and discussions. Attendees were invited to explore various critical topics, including processes, IT systems, and IT security. The forum’s agenda was packed with engaging keynotes on the digitalization of engineering and insightful talks on security-related and strategic topics from Volkswagen.
Raphael Heilig, Managing Director of Teraport, expressed his excitement about participating in the event, stating, " Volkswagen Development Partner Forum provides an unparalleled platform for us to engage with industry leaders and discuss the latest developments and standards. It's an invaluable opportunity to collaborate and share our innovative solutions with key players in the automotive sector." The insights gained and connections made during the event will inform future developments, ensuring that Teraport continues to deliver cutting-edge solutions that address the evolving needs of the industry.
