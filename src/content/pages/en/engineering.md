<strong>Complete vehicle development</strong> – Our engineers support the complete vehicle development from concept to series and enhancements. Within the scope of our work we take responsibility for technical solutions and their clearance between the different technical stakeholders. Among our assignements are the lead and management of meetings and reporting to the different positions within the matrix organisation.

<strong>Qualitymanagement</strong> - Our activities in the field of quality assurance contain development, tracking, documentation and reporting of quality goals. This includes different methods of escalation in case of deviations. Long term experience enables Teraport's consultants to identify qualitay risks within minimum time and initiate prevention measures. Our customers value us as the quality assurance link between development and production.

<strong>Datamanagement and virtual car</strong> - Teraport's employess are experts in product data management (PDM) of complex virtual products. To set up product development environments enabling the availability of the right data in the right configuration along the whole product lifecycle is one of the core challenges of producing industry. Aside from vast knowledge of the software tools experience with the processes and needs of the users is the base for a succesful PDM set up. The synergy between software and engineering allow our cosultants to offer high quality expertise to our customers.

<strong>Methodology development CAx</strong> - For over 15 years Teraport is active in CAx methodology development within the automotive industry. By a broad knowledge of our customers processes our consultants achieve, after a profound analysis, the saving of valuable time during the development process and the deployment of new methodology during running projects. Through many long term projects with our customers, we accumulated knowledge in terms of automatisation, skripting, macros and many more.
