<blockquote><em>veo</em></blockquote>

By <em>veo</em> Teraport offers its customers a highly capable 3D-viewer. Its focus is on fast visualization of large assemblies. A large number of CAD interfaces makes sure virtualy every model is readable and displayable. Aside from basic functionality like measuring and sectioning <em>veo</em> offers a wide range of additional features in the fields of DMU, analysis, calculation and simulation. These are bundled in optional workbenches. The specialty: Ambitionous customers are able to have  individual functionality integrated in a customer specific workbench allowing full interaction with the 3D Models in the viewport. The special concept of <em>veo's</em> modular architecture allows this in a fast yet robust manner. A concept proved in its application within representative customers many times.
